Report Preview

What the final report looks like.

The goal is not to overwhelm you with technical language. The goal is to show what matters, why it matters, and what to fix first.

Sample Finding

Missing Security Headers

Medium Severity

Business Impact

Missing browser security controls may increase exposure to client-side attacks, reduce trust, and make the website appear less professionally maintained.

Evidence

Header review shows missing or incomplete protections such as Content-Security-Policy, X-Frame-Options, and other browser-side controls.

Recommended Fix

Add recommended security headers through the hosting provider, CDN, or web server configuration. Re-test after implementation.

Priority

Fix within 30 days unless the website handles sensitive customer data, payments, or account logins.

Sample Log Finding

Repeated Suspicious Activity

High Severity

Business Impact

Repeated suspicious activity from the same source may indicate scanning, login probing, or automated attempts to find weak areas.

Evidence

Log records show repeated requests, denied connections, or failed login attempts from the same IP address or time window.

Recommended Fix

Review hosting, firewall, Cloudflare, or access control settings. Add alerting, rate limiting, or blocking rules where appropriate.

Priority

Review immediately if activity involves admin pages, login pages, payment systems, or customer data.

FAQ

Questions business owners usually ask.

Is this a penetration test?

No. This is a passive external security readiness review. It focuses on visible risk, trust issues, exposure, and practical remediation. No exploitation or unauthorized access is performed.

What is the mini-offer for?

The Mini Website Risk Snapshot is for businesses that want a low-cost first look before paying for a deeper security review.

What is log review in simple terms?

Logs are records of what happened on a website, server, firewall, or hosting account. I review those records to look for repeated suspicious activity, unusual IPs, failed login patterns, or signs that something needs more attention.

Do I need to be technical?

No. The report is written for business owners. You will get plain-English explanations, screenshots where useful, severity ratings, and practical next steps.

Why does the main package cost $1.6K to $2.5K?

The price reflects the depth of review, evidence collection, business-risk analysis, report quality, remediation roadmap, and follow-up call.

What do I need to send?

Send your website link, business type, main concern, and deadline. If requesting log review, you can also send exported logs from your hosting provider, firewall, Cloudflare, WordPress security plugin, or server dashboard.
